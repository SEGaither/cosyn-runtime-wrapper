# CoSyn Origin Record

Author: Shane Gaither

This repository records the architectural design and development history
of the CoSyn reasoning governance system and its associated Runtime
Wrapper (RTW).

The artifacts within the Knowledgebase directory establish a timestamped
public record of:

-   the CoSyn constitutional governance model
-   the governance stack architecture
-   the Runtime Wrapper concept and deployment strategy
-   the design evolution of the system

Publication of these artifacts in a public repository creates a
verifiable historical record of the system's conceptual development and
authorship.
