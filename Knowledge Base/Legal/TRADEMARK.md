# CoSyn Trademark Policy

## Ownership

The name **CoSyn** and related marks are the intellectual property of the project creator.

All rights are reserved.

## Allowed Uses

You may:

- Refer to CoSyn in documentation
- Describe compatibility with CoSyn
- Reference the project in academic or research work

## Restricted Uses

You may not:

- Use "CoSyn" in the name of a competing product
- Imply official affiliation
- Create confusingly similar product names
- Misrepresent modified forks as official CoSyn releases

## Fork Naming

Forks must use names that clearly distinguish them from the original project.

Example:

Allowed:
- "ProjectX powered by CoSyn"

Not allowed:
- "CoSyn Enterprise"
- "Official CoSyn Fork"

## Enforcement

The project owner reserves the right to enforce trademark protection where misuse causes confusion or brand dilution.
